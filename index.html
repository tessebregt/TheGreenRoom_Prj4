By day, we’re a cozy café serving coffee, breakfast, and lunch, a space to meet, create, or unwind. By night, a lively jazz bar and theatre hub, where music and performance bring people together.

Whether you’re here to perform, watch, or simply soak in the atmosphere, The Green Room is a space where creativity thrives, connections spark, and every voice is heard. Plus, with affordable drinks and event tickets, we make sure the arts are for everyone.

Step inside, take a seat, and let the story unfold!
