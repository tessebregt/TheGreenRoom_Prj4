We Are

Menu

Welcome ladies and gentlemen to an environment where moments, connections, and personal stories naturally take place, one cup at a time. Step inside, take a seat, and let the story unfold.

The Green Room is your go-to spot to get together for theater lovers, creatives, and anyone who enjoys a great story.

By day, we’re a cozy café, perfect for sipping coffee, reading scripts, or sharing ideas with like-minded souls. By night, we transform into a lively theater bar, serving themed cocktails alongside open mic nights, improv shows, cabaret performances, and immersive experiences. There’s always something happening, and you’re always invited.

Whether you’re here to perform, watch, or simply soak in the atmosphere, The Green Room is a space where creativity thrives, connections spark, and every voice is heard. Plus, with affordable drinks and event tickets, we make sure the arts are for everyone.

So come in, get comfortable, and let’s make some unforgettable memories together!
