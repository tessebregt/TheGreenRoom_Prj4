About Us


Hi fellow theatre lover.

Welcome to the green room, our cozy backstage escape where the drinks flow, the stories shine, and everyone’s part of the cast. We’re four passionate theatre fans who believe every performance, from improv nights to Broadway hits, deserves its moment in the spotlight.
Here, you can sip on a limited edition cocktail or coffee, swap stories, and connect with fellow theatre lovers. Whether you're making new friends or catching up with old ones, this is a warm, welcoming space where you’ll always feel like part of the family.

Our values

Everybody deserves to belong 

At The Green Room, everyone is welcome. We’re a creative community where all voices are valued, and the arts are for everyone. This is your space to belong.

Lasting moments

At The Green Room, we create real moments and lasting connections through shared, immersive experiences that bring people together in meaningful ways.

Fueling stories, igniting voices

The Green Room sparks creativity and connection through stories, performances, and everyday moments. Every voice matters, and everyone contributes to the experience.

Creativity knows no boundaries

At The Green Room, creativity knows no boundaries. We provide a space where ideas grow, risks are embraced, and expression is free. Here, you’re encouraged to create without limits.
