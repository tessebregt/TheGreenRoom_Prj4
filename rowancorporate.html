Content

Vision:

To build an inspiring environment where ideas grow, relationships deepen, and stories find their voices. A place where creativity has no limits and every moment sparks new possibilities.

Values:

Everybody Deserves to Belong:

We believe that every person, regardless of background, experience, or identity, deserves a place to call their own, a space where they feel seen, valued, and understood. Whether you're a lifelong theater lover or just discovering the magic of the stage, here, you belong. This is a place where creativity knows no boundaries, where voices from all walks of life can find harmony, and where every individual is welcomed with open arms. We’re making the arts accessible for everyone.

Lasting Moments:

We believe that some experiences are meant to be felt, not just seen. It's not just about watching a performance or enjoying a drink, it's about creating moments that linger, stories that stay with you long after the curtain falls. Whether it's a spontaneous improvisation show, a heartwarming open mic night, or an immersive theater experience, we aim to create connections that last.

Fueling Stories, Igniting Voices:

We believe in the power of stories to transform and connect. From a whispered line in a script to the roar of applause at the end of a performance, every voice has the potential to spark something incredible. We're here to fuel that spark, whether it's over a quiet cup of coffee, a casual script reading, or a night of live entertainment.

Creativity Knows No Boundaries:

We believe creativity is not confined by walls or rules, it’s a force that knows no boundaries. It flows freely, uninhibited, and we’re here to support that freedom at every turn. Whether you’re an actor stepping into character, a writer breathing life into a new script, or someone enjoying the magic of live performance, we’ve created a space where creativity is celebrated, encouraged, and unleashed.

Brand Name:

We came up with the brand name The Green Room, which refers to the backstage lounge where actors wait before performing.

Visual Identity:

General style:

Logo:
The Green Room's logo features an elegant, vintage-inspired symbol with a coffee cup at the center, symbolizing the café atmosphere during the day. Below it, theatrical masks are highlighting the theater theme and live entertainment aspect of the brand. This gives the logo a classic, refined look and reflects the artistic and creative energy of the space. The design aligns with our mission of creating a warm, inspiring space where communities can gather.

Color Palette:
We spent a long time searching for the perfect balance, colors that capture the warmth and creativity of the café during the day, as well as the intimate and theatrical ambiance of the bar at night.
Together, these (..show..) colors create a welcoming environment for theater lovers to gather. The mix of deep greens and reds reflect the traditional aesthetic of historic theatres, while the browns and creams provide a welcoming touch.
As the primary color we chose red.

Typography:
We chose Berkshire Swash for our brand because it has a stylish, vintage look, like the lettering on classic theater signs and playbills. It has just the right mix of fancy details and easy-to-read letters, making it both elegant and inviting.

Iconography:
In our branding, we’ll include small theater-inspired details like spotlights, vintage tickets, and decorative script accents. The graphics will be stylish but not too complicated, blending artistic with a clean look.

Photography Style:
Our photography captures the energy of the theater world. We focus on candid, in-the-moment shots that showcase real interactions. Low contrast and warm tones will capture the nostalgic, inviting feel of the space. A mix of cozy café shots during the day and moody, atmospheric event photos at night will visually express the transformation of the space.

Website and App Design:

Layouts and Composition:
The website and digital materials have a clear, structured layout while adding playful, theatrical details like playbills and ornate frames. This design keeps things organized while still feeling creative and immersive. The text is styled for easy reading, with elegant headers and simple, modern body text for clarity. The layout is simple but engaging, making it easy to explore while keeping things interactive. At the top, our slogan invites visitors: “Step inside, take a seat, and let the story unfold.” The menu adds to the theme. Fun features like an event schedule, social media updates, and easy booking make the site more interactive. It also works smoothly on both computers and phones, so users can browse from anywhere. Flyers, posters, and menus match the vintage theater theme, using decorative designs, classic serif fonts, and rich, deep colors to strengthen the brand’s identity.

Tone of Voice:
Our language is warm, informal and inviting, we want visitors to feel like they’re talking to a fellow theater lover.
We want to use playful theatrical references to keep messaging fun and immersive (e.g., “Take center stage with our signature cocktails” or “Join us for an encore-worthy performance”). Storytelling is key. Whether describing an event or our menu, we use narrative elements to create an emotional connection with our audience. Accessibility and inclusivity are central, our messaging ensures that both seasoned theatergoers and newcomers feel equally welcome.

Usability Testing Report:

Testing Protocol:

Target Users: Ages 25–50, frequent theater visitors or café-goers

Methodology: In-person usability tests and remote screen-sharing sessions

Tasks Assigned:
  • Book a ticket to a show
  • Find the menu
  • Navigate to upcoming events
  • Use contact form to ask a question
Testing Goals:
  • Evaluate the clarity of the navigation
  • Test mobile responsiveness
  • Determine how effectively the website communicates its dual café-theatre identity
Key Findings:
  • Users found the dual identity of the café/theatre well integrated
  • Navigation was mostly intuitive but some users overlooked the café section
  • Event carousel generated high engagement but loaded slowly on mobile
  • Booking process was smooth, but the confirmation email was marked as spam by one tester
Improvements Based on Findings:
  • Add a visual icon or highlight to the Café tab for better visibility
  • Optimize carousel loading time for mobile
  • Ensure booking confirmation email passes spam filters
  • Consider adding breadcrumbs for deeper subpages like event details or menus

Marketing

Target Audience:

Our target audience spans a diverse age range, from students (16-25) to passionate theatre professionals and enthusiasts (25-50+). They are found primarily in urban and cultural hubs such as Amsterdam, Rotterdam, The Hague, and Utrecht, as well as medium-sized cities like Groningen, Eindhoven, and Maastricht, where theatre culture thrives. They come from a mix of income backgrounds, with students seeking affordability and professionals willing to invest in premium experiences. Theatre lovers are typically engaged with the arts, whether through their academic pursuits, creative careers, or lifelong passion for theatre, storytelling, music, and literature.

Demographics:

  • Age: Ranges from students (16-25) to theatre professionals and enthusiasts (25-50+).
  • Location: Primarily urban and cultural hubs with strong arts scenes.
  • Income: Varies: students seeking affordability, professionals willing to invest in premium experiences.
  • Education: Theatre students, creatives, and lifelong arts enthusiasts.

Psychographics:

Theatre lovers value creativity, inclusivity, emotional connection, and self-expression. They enjoy frequent attendance at live performances, participate in theatre workshops, and stay engaged with theatre communities. However, they face challenges such as limited access to performances, high ticket costs, and lack of diverse representation in theatre. Despite these pain points, their motivations are driven by a desire for immersive experiences, emotional connections with stories and performers, and support for creative expression.

Behavior and Engagement:

  • Theatre Consumption: Attend live performances, subscribe to streaming platforms, engage in theatre workshops and festivals.
  • Online Presence: Active on Instagram, TikTok, theatre blogs, Reddit, YouTube, and arts-focused newsletters.
  • Spending Habits: Invest in tickets, season passes, theatre-related merchandise, and exclusive content.
  • Community Involvement: Participate in discussions, follow theatre companies and actors, join fan groups.

Competitor and Market Landscape:

The Dutch theatre industry consists of two primary sectors: subsidized and non-subsidized (commercial) theatre. The subsidized sector focuses on artistic innovation and culturally relevant productions, while the commercial sector offers large-scale, financially viable productions. Both sectors engage audiences with distinct branding strategies, either focusing on artistic integrity or entertainment value. Our campaign aims to fill the gap by creating an inclusive and creative space for all theatre lovers.

  • Community Focus: Emphasis on inclusivity, fostering loyalty, and word-of-mouth promotion.
Weaknesses:
  • Niche Market Appeal: The specialized theme may limit broader audience reach.
  • Resource Intensive: Diverse services and events require significant staffing and operational resources.
Opportunities:
  • Cultural Collaborations: Partnering with local artists and theatres can enhance visibility.
  • Themed Events: Hosting open mic nights and book readings can attract diverse crowds.
  • Merchandising: Selling themed merchandise can create additional revenue streams.
Threats:
  • Economic Downturns: Financial instability can affect discretionary spending.
  • Competition: Other entertainment venues and cafés may offer similar experiences.
  • Changing Customer Preferences: The rise of digital media may impact patronage.

Engagement and Community Building:

At The Green Room, we aim to create a space where both our audience and our community feel valued and connected. Through a series of initiatives and interactive experiences, we will build long-term relationships that go beyond just transactions and evolve into genuine connections.

  • Interactive Events and Performances:
    • Themed Nights and Collaborations: We will host regular themed nights, such as "Open Mic Theatre," "Jazz and Stories," or "Poetry Slams," where patrons can share their talents. These nights will create a platform for our audience to express themselves and engage with the arts.
    • Exclusive Backstage Access: Offering behind-the-scenes access to rehearsals or special meet-and-greets with performers gives our community an inside look into the creative process, enhancing their connection with the performers and the space.
  • Social Media Engagement:
    • Interactive Polls and Challenges: We will launch social media polls, quizzes, and challenges (e.g., "Which character would you play in our next show?") to encourage participation and spark conversation among our followers. This content will invite our audience to become a part of our storytelling process.
    • User-Generated Content Campaigns: By encouraging customers to share photos or videos of their experiences at The Green Room, we can celebrate the creative contributions of our community. We'll create a branded hashtag, such as #GreenRoomStories, and feature the best submissions on our social media channels.
  • Loyalty Programs and Exclusive Rewards:
    • VIP Membership: A loyalty program offering members access to discounted tickets, exclusive events, and special merchandise will foster a sense of belonging. Members will also receive personalized recommendations based on their preferences.
    • Rewards for Engagement: For patrons who actively participate in our community-building activities, such as sharing their own creative work or attending multiple events, we will offer tangible rewards, such as free tickets, drink vouchers, or first access to new events.
  • Community Outreach and Local Collaborations:
    • Workshops and Classes: We will host acting, storytelling, and creative writing workshops to give our community the opportunity to nurture their skills and meet others who share their passions. These workshops will help The Green Room become not just a café, but a cultural hub for growth and connection.
    • Partnerships with Local Organizations: By teaming up with local charities, schools, and other cultural venues, we can extend our reach and integrate deeper into the local community. Collaborative events, such as fundraising performances or charity workshops, will show our commitment to supporting the arts while giving back to those in need.
  • Personalized Customer Interaction:
    • Tailored Communication: Through our email newsletters, we’ll send personalized content that resonates with individual interests, such as recommendations for upcoming events based on previous visits. This personal touch will help build stronger bonds with our audience.
    • Customer Feedback Loops: We’ll create opportunities for our patrons to share their ideas and feedback through surveys, suggestion boxes, and social media interactions. This feedback will not only shape our offerings but also give our community a voice in shaping The Green Room’s future.

By fostering an environment where creativity, collaboration, and connection thrive, we’ll build a loyal and engaged community that feels an integral part of The Green Room’s story. This engagement will not only help us increase foot traffic but also encourage repeat visits and enthusiastic word-of-mouth recommendations, turning each patron into a long-term advocate for our brand.
